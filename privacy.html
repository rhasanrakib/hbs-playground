Introduction

Welcome to Handlebars Template Renderer ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our online Handlebars template rendering service.

Key Point: Your templates and data are processed entirely in your browser. We do not store or transmit your code to our servers.

Information We Collect

1. Information You Provide

  • Template Content: Handlebars templates you input into our editor
  • JSON Data: Data you provide for template rendering
  • Uploaded Files: .hbs and .json files you choose to upload

2. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics (renders, formats, validations)
  • Technical Information: Browser type, version, and viewport size
  • Performance Data: Page load times and error rates

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to:

  • Save your current work automatically
  • Remember your theme preferences
  • Store anonymous usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide template rendering functionality
  • Improvement: To enhance and optimize our service
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our tool
  • Technical Support: To identify and fix technical issues

Data Processing and Storage

Important: All template rendering occurs locally in your browser. Your code never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.

Local Processing

  • Templates are compiled using Handlebars.js in your browser
  • JSON data is parsed and validated locally
  • Rendering happens entirely client-side

Storage Locations

  • Browser Local Storage (your device)
  • Browser Session Storage (temporary)
  • No server-side storage of your templates or data

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • CDN Services: For loading CodeMirror and Handlebars libraries
  • Analytics: Basic usage tracking (optional)
  • Hosting: Static file hosting through our web server

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Content Security Policy headers
  • Regular security updates
  • Secure coding practices

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of analytics tracking

Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage data (optional)

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your data is processed locally in your browser. CDN resources may be served from global locations, but contain no personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, when feasible, through other notification methods.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@hbsplayground.xyz
  • GitHub Issues: For technical questions

Summary: We respect your privacy. Your code stays on your device, and we only collect minimal data to improve the service.
